About Us

Our Company

Sam's agency, online service (freelance) organization was founded by Joseph Sam 2018, to reach small businesses and start up businesses, which can't afford the high prices for web developing & designing, branding and marketing. We thought that it was a good idea to start small business marketing, but we had no funds to start. So we figured we should start designing website, which will be a major milestone for marketing of the clients i.e: getting clients, doing online transactions, sharing business information like location, prices , etc, easing the access of customers, cheap advertising, and many more.

We work as a team of several skilled individuals determined in doing the best at our work. With several completed projects, we have built our reputation with several local business eg, andikaus Hotels, Hillside real estate,etc, from simple log in page to a busy e-comerce website to a simple software (a payee calculator, a program that deletes all music files, among others).


For the last three month we have improved from being a one service team to several service team, we really appreciate. We have now widened our area of specialization from a simple web designing team to web development & graphic design. We have been able to introduce new members in our team specialized in graphic designing. Soon afterwards we are planning to expand our "territory" and start services like video editing, 3D modelling & rendering and 3D mapping
